InnovateHub 2024

Join us for a 48-hour hackathon where creativity meets technology. Build, innovate, and win!

About

InnovateHub is an annual hackathon bringing together developers, designers, and innovators to tackle real-world challenges. This year's theme is 'Tech for Good,' focusing on solutions that address social and environmental issues. Participants will have access to mentors, workshops, and resources to help bring their ideas to life.

Problem Statements

This year's hackathon focuses on the theme 'Tech for Good,' with problem statements centered around social and environmental impact. Participants can choose from challenges in areas such as sustainable living, education accessibility, and community empowerment. Detailed problem statements will be released one week before the event.

Tracks

InnovateHub offers three tracks to cater to diverse interests and skill levels: 1. **Sustainable Solutions:** Develop tech solutions for environmental sustainability, such as renewable energy management, waste reduction, and conservation efforts. 2. **Accessible Education:** Create tools and platforms that enhance educational opportunities for underserved communities, promoting inclusivity and lifelong learning. 3. **Community Empowerment:** Design applications that foster community engagement, improve local services, and address social challenges within neighborhoods.
